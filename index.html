01

Execute. Iterate. Ruthlessly improve.

I spent thirty years building systems that turn data into decisions. The first sporting goods store to accept a credit card online. A decentralized warehouse supply chain logistics model before Amazon had one. Then Scale AI — Senior QM of the Code Division, managing the PhDs, engineers, and scholars who built the training data behind OpenAI, Google, Meta, Anthropic, Cursor, Alibaba Qwen, X AI, and Tesla.

When we completed the most critical project in the company's history — the engagement that directly led to Meta's $19 billion investment — they let my entire team go. The admin team had nicknamed me The Savior of Scale. It didn't matter.

I was heartbroken. Not because I lost a job. Because I lost the team I'd built and the mission we shared. My life's work has been a single pursuit — accessing industrial and private data to make better business decisions and stay competitive. That pursuit didn't end. It never will.

03

Intelligence frameworks for risk mitigation

reconλis provides intelligence frameworks for the industries where risk is the product, the portfolio, and the constraint. Our systems unify fragmented data into continuous, actionable intelligence — turning what was invisible into what is inevitable.

Insurance

Acquisition, valuation, and operational optimization of independent insurance agencies. AI-driven portfolio intelligence built on a unified ontology of financial, policy, claims, and customer data. Risk identification before it becomes loss.

Private Equity

End-to-end deal automation from sourcing through earnout. Continuous valuation models that capture relationship equity traditional multiples miss. Retention-weighted performance tracking that surfaces risk across portfolio companies in real time.

Hedge Funds

Premium routing, float optimization, and proprietary underwriting powered by operational data from owned portfolio companies. An information advantage for alternative investment strategies that compounds with every transaction.
